
Welcome to Simpson College Pet Shelter
======================================

Welcome!
--------
Welcome to Simpson College Pet Shelter. Here you will find
information on the process of adopting a new furry friend!
As you navigate through our site, you will notice we offer 
help with the adoption process, how to get involved with our 
shelther, tips and tricks on training your new pet, infomraiton
on vaccinations and other pet-health topics, our fostering
process, and the events that we host here at our shelther!

We are so glad you are here, and look forward to working with you!


Our Purpose
-----------
Changing the life of animals in need.
Simpson College Pet Shelther serves people and pets from central
Iowa through pet adoption, pet education, behavior training, vet
resources, and the fostering process.
